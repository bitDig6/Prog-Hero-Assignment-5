Frequently Asked Questions

How should I make the donations?

State your donation amount in the box and click on the 'donate now' button.

How much donation should I make?

Make donations according to your available balance.

How do I know my donation was made successfully?

You'll receive a notification on the website.

Can I make donations more than my available balance?

No. At first, you have to add balance to your account following the proper payment methods.
